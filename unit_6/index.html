Your guide to buying a property on Mars

Are you bored of earth? Tired of animals dying out and oceans too polluted to swim in? It is time to look for new opportunities. With oceans rising and populations becoming displaced land has never been so valuable. Mars is this months hot property tip and we have prepared a guide just for you. You are welcome!

1.Be a first mover

Buying property on Mars has never been cheaper. And with the extreme weather patterns of earth disrupting food production, now has never been a better time to buy. Once you have adapted to lower oxygen levels and extreme isolation you will be sure to enjoy 7% return per year on your investment.

2.Make a plan

Decide on what is important to you, then build a strategy. For most of this is about survival followed by schools and a great neighbourhood. Once you have made a list you will know where to compromise so you can make a quick decision or move to another option.

3.Get finance ready

You will have more negotiation power if you have a pre-approved mortgage. The current relocation fee for the colony on Mars was around 160 billion dollars. Speak to your bank manager and create a saving plan for any additional money your require.

The earth will become uninhabitable for humans. Climate change, excessive waste and pollution are some of the many challenges we face. While options might be available we are not working fast enough to tackle these issues.

See things in a different way. Understand the urgency. Take action.
